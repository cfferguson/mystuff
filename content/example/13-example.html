If you want to take a look at ANOVA+1, try giving this video a go. It discusses an idea called covariance which takes ANOVAS to ANCOVAS.

There are also more complex models called MANOVA, MANCOVAS, and so forth. Always keep in mind that as the complexity of a statistical model goes up, the ability to apply it generally goes down! If you want a rundown of the differing types, take a look at this page over at Stats Make Me Cry Consulting2.

In a nutshell, most problems that require inferences can be solved using a t-test or \(\chi^2\). Keep it simple and accessible!
